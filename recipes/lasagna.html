Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious lasagna that will leave you deppressed once you finish it, it can be done in 30 minutes of prep and one hour of baking time, this recipe is simple and can be broken down into easy steps that anyone can make it!

The igredients needed are

  • 150 grams sheets lasgna
  • 800 grams of pork and beef 50/50
  • 500 grams of mascarpone
  • 200 grams of mozzarella
  • 150 grams of parmegiano regiano
  • 800 grams of crushed tomatoes
  • 80 grams of tomato pasta
  • 1 egg
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tablespoons of oil

Steps

  1. Start by boiling the pasta ( if not ready to bake)
  2. Now add 2tbs oil to a pan
  3. Add your minced meat to a pan and cook for 8 minutes or until a light brown
  4. After the meat reached a light brown put it aside
  5. Mince the garlic and cook until brown
  6. Add chopped onions and cook for 3 minutes
  7. Once the onions are golden add your tomato pasta
  8. After 1 minute add the crushed tomatoes
  9. It's time to add salt, pepper and your spices of choice to taste
  10. Now add back your minced meat and be sure that it's in the smallest of chunks
  11. After 3 minutes, the fun part begins
  12. Prepare your baking pan, add first a layer of sauce it should be 1/4 of the sauce
  13. Followed by pasta that covers the sauce as best as possible
  14. We add our mascarpone now to coat the pasta lightly
  15. After which we add a 1/3 of the cheese
  16. Add sauce, pasta, mascarpone and cheese 1 more time
  17. This time, we will add our sauce pasta mascarpone cheese, we go with the sauce and cheese to cover it
  18. Add a baking sheet to cover it and bake for 25 minutes
  19. Once the 25 minutes are up, we remove the sheet and we bake it for 35 more minutes
  20. After which, we let it cool for 15 minutes before cutting it with a knife
